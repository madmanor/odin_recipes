Marry Me Chicken

What is Marry Me Chicken?

Maybe you have heard of Marry Me Chicken before, a delicious dish of sautèed chicken
blanketed in a creamy sun-dried tomato sauce that "is worthy of a marriage proposal!"
It can be enjoyed on its own or served over pasta or rice for an extra hearty meal. But it
can also be turned into an irresistible soup for a perfect bowl of comfort all in one pot.

How to Make Marry Me Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound boneless chicken(recommended chicken breasts)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves, minched garlic
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon ground thyme
  • ½ cup chicken broth, divided
  • ½ pound bacon
  • 16 ounces pasta(recommended angel hair)
  • 1/2 pound bacon
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1/4 cup whipping cream
  • 1/4 sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt to taste
